F1 Cost Cap Compliance Assistant

✓ Output cross-checked 89% citation rate, up from 31% baseline

A hybrid-retrieval RAG system over FIA Formula 1 financial regulations, with an ablation study isolating exactly what a grounded prompt vs. hybrid retrieval each contribute. Stats & Deep Learning

LendingClub Loan Analysis

✓ Re-run verified $375,985 revenue edge from a risk-averse policy — with a catch

A/B test of two loan-approval policies (ANOVA + Welch t-tests), a hybrid hierarchical/K-means borrower segmentation, and a PyTorch default-prediction model. Independently re-run against the real ~50,000-loan dataset — every financial figure and statistical test matched the original report. Cluster-level analysis overturns the simple "stricter policy wins" headline: it only wins for two of the three segments.

  • R
  • PyTorch
  • Hypothesis Testing
  • Clustering
